Racial Traits:
Ability Score Increase: Ironhand's ability scores each increase by 1.
Extra Language: Ironhand can speak, read, and write one extra language of your choice.

Class Features:
Fighting Style (Great Weapon Fighting): When Ironhand rolls a 1 or 2 on a damage die for an attack he makes with a melee weapon that he is wielding with two hands, he can reroll the die and must use the new roll.
Second Wind: Ironhand has a limited well of stamina that he can draw on to protect himself from harm.
Action Surge: On his turn, Ironhand can take one additional action.
Indomitable: Ironhand can reroll a saving throw that he fails.
Extra Attack (3): Ironhand can attack three times, instead of once, whenever he takes the Attack action on his turn.
Martial Archetype (Champion): Ironhand's martial training allows him to perform remarkable feats of combat capability and endurance.

Spells: Fighters do not have spellcasting abilities, so this is not applicable for Ironhand.

Equipment: Plate armor, Greatsword, Longbow with 20 arrows, a set of dice, an insignia of rank, a trophy taken from a fallen enemy (a dagger, broken blade, or piece of a banner), a set of bone dice or deck of cards, a set of common clothes, a belt pouch containing 10 gp.
